About DLR

DLR Business Solution Pvt. Ltd., legally registered on 1st June 2025, is the parent company behind DLR Training Institute & Recruitment. Our head office is located in Panjim, Goa. At DLR Training Institute & Recruitment, we believe everyone deserves a chance to build a successful and rewarding career. Our mission is to provide practical, job-oriented training that equips students with the skills and confidence to excel in the glamorous world of casinos.

Whether you are a student, a working professional, or seeking a career change, DLR offers expert-designed courses that combine hands-on training with real-world knowledge. We emphasize both technical skills and essential soft skills, ensuring our students are fully prepared for the casino floor and beyond.

With a focus on quality education, industry-relevant training, and student success, DLR Training Institute & Recruitment is your gateway to a promising career in the casino industry.
